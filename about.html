About

Runtime State is a research archive where I publish analysis and field notes on South African technology, policy, and governance.

Method

The primary methods are discourse analysis and incident mapping. Policy documents are read not for what they propose but for what they reveal about the assumptions, silences, and structural logics that shape them. Cyber incidents are compiled from public records, annual reports, and ministerial disclosures to surface systemic patterns.
